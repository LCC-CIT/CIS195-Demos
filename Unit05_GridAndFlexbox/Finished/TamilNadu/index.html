Tamil Nadu

About Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is the southernmost state in India. It is the tenth largest state by area and the sixth largest by population. The capital and largest city is Chennai. Tamil Nadu is the home of the Tamil people, whose language is one of the longest surviving classical languages in the world. (Paraphrased from Wikipedia.)
